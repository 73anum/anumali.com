Research Interests

I am broadly interested in signal processing for wireless communications. Some particular topics that I have focused on are:

Beyond 5G and 6G

  • 5G UE power saving [J21]
  • Beam management challenges in 6G communications [J18]

Millimeter Wave Sensing

  • Micro gesture recognition [J20]

Millimeter Wave Communication

  • MmWave communication assisted by infrastructure mounted sensors [J13]
  • Sub-6 GHz channel information to configure mmWave links [J8], [J9], [J11], [C7], [C8]
  • Radar data to configure V2X mmWave links [C11], [C12], [C13], [J15]
  • Orientation assisted mmWave beam management [J17], [C17]
  • MmWave covariance estimation [J12], [C10]
  • 5G V2X rate maps [C14]
  • Radar and communication coexistence [C15]
  • Beam codebook design [C16]

Non-stationary Massive MIMO Systems

  • Analysis of linear precoders [J10]
  • Challenges and directions [J14]

Applications of Compressed Sensing

  • THz channel estimation [J22], [C18], [C19]
  • Narrowband interference mitigation [J7], [C4]
  • Peak-to-average power ratio (PAPR) reduction [J5], [C2], [C3], [C6]
  • Power amplifier linearization [J2], [J3], [P1]

Adaptive Filters

  • Design and steady state excess mean-square-error (EMSE) analysis of blind filters [J1], [J4], [C1]
  • Analysis of normalized least mean squares (NLMS) algorithm [J16], [J19]
